    The Hive

    Hi there, welcome! President B here, thanks for dropping by. We are a non-profit organization that works together with environmental and agricultural scientists, to help improve and sustain the lives of our bees. We stand proudly behind our little friends, because they play a HUGE role in cultivating our crops. It is vital that we protect these creatures at all costs so that they can pollinate our crops and plants! Our Mission is to teach others about the importance of bees, and how to love them from afar.
